Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, we prioritize response times.

Can I contribute to HashFlow Pro's development?

Yes! We're open to contributions and collaborations. If you're a developer interested in contributing to our open-source components or have ideas for collaboration, please use the partnership inquiry option.

Do you offer educational resources or workshops?

We regularly publish educational content on our blog and social media channels. For custom workshops or educational partnerships, please contact us through the partnership inquiry form.

How do I report a bug or technical issue?

Please use the Technical Support option in the contact form above. Include as much detail as possible about the issue, including your browser, device, and steps to reproduce the problem.
