Usage Tips & Best Practices

The optimal number varies by platform for a balanced strategy:

  • Instagram: You can use up to 30, but 8-15 highly relevant hashtags is often most effective.
  • Twitter / X: 1-3 hashtags is recommended due to character limits.
  • Facebook: 3-5 hashtags is generally sufficient.
  • TikTok: 3-5 popular and niche hashtags can help with discoverability.

Focus on relevance and quality over quantity. Our AI tool helps you find the right balance.

Effective hashtags are:

  • Relevant: Directly related to your content and audience.
  • Specific: Mix popular, broad hashtags with more niche ones to reach targeted users.
  • Readable: Use clear phrasing (e.g., #SocialMediaTips not #socialmediatips).
  • Current: Leverage trending topics when appropriate.

Our tools are designed to help you create hashtags that meet these criteria.
